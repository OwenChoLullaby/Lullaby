<span class="inp-label req">항목1</span> <div class="input-wrap search"> <input type="text" title="첨부파일명 입력" placeholder="파일명을 입력하세요." class="ui-input" aria-controls="toast-msg01"> <button type="button" class="btn-del">삭제</button> <button type="button" class="btn-search">검색</button> </div> <!-- ui-toast --> <div class="ui-toast" aria-live="toast-msg01"> 첨부파일명은 필수 입력항목입니다. </div> <!-- //ui-toast -->

<ul class="ui-check mt20 mb20" aria-controls="toast-msg02"> <li><input type="radio" id="radio01-1" name="radio01"><label for="radio01-1">동의합니다.</label></li> <li><input type="radio" id="radio01-2" name="radio01"><label for="radio01-2">동의하지않습니다.</label></li> </ul> <!-- ui-toast --> <div class="ui-toast" aria-live="toast-msg02"> 필수 선택항목을 선택해주세요. </div> <!-- //ui-toast -->
